# Roadmap

## Current Focus

- Continue refining the workflow from a single idea to storyboard, images, and video
- Continue strengthening image consistency and asset-centered creation
- Continue improving how video results are generated, surfaced, and reused

## Next

- Clearer public presentation of the core workbench workflow
- More complete role / scene / prop showcase cases
- Stronger narrative storyboard demonstrations
- Better history, recall, and reuse presentation

## Longer Term

- Higher-quality collaboration between image and video workflows
- More mature long-form continuous creation experience
- Selectively open or demonstrate a limited subset of basic capabilities when the product is mature enough

## Out of Scope

This public roadmap does not include:

- business cooperation details
- training plans
- private deployment delivery details
- proxy compatibility or fallback strategy
- backend designs that would make the full product easy to replicate
